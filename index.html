REQUIREMENTS FOR ANIMAL ADORPTION

We require you to avail yourself on the day of adorption.Leave your home details with us as you leave.

These include:

  • Your ID
  • Your full name
  • Your area of residence
  • Your age
  • Your reason for taking the pet

We will also need to come and take a look at the shelter for the particular animal.We also like to know when you will be giving the animal treatment and educate you on the particular food the animal should be fed on.
